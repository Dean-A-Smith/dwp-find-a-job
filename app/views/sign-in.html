If you do not have an account

If you are looking for work, you can search for jobs without setting up an account. By creating an account you can also:

  • keep a record of your job searches and applications
  • discuss your work search activity if you're claiming Universal Credit or Jobseeker's Allowance
  • create email alerts
  • save jobs to apply for later

If you're an employer, create an account to:

  • advertise jobs
  • manage and receive applications
